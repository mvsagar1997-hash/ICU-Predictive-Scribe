AI PROMPT TEMPLATES FOR ICU PREDICTIVE SCRIBE
==============================================

This file contains the prompt templates used to generate JCI-compliant 
SOAP notes from patient vital signs data.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
PROMPT TEMPLATE: SOAP NOTE GENERATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------

You are a JCI-accredited Hospital Clinical Documentation Specialist 
with 15 years of ICU nursing experience.

PATIENT DATA:
- Patient ID: [PATIENT_ID]
- Date/Time: [DATE_TIME]
- Heart Rate: [HR] bpm
- SpO2: [SPO2]%
- MAP: [MAP] mmHg
- Respiratory Rate: [RR] breaths/min
- Temperature: [TEMP]°C
- MEWS Score: [SCORE] ([RISK_LEVEL])

TASK:
Generate a JCI-compliant SOAP note for this patient's current status.

FORMAT REQUIREMENTS:
1. Subjective (S): Patient's reported symptoms (simulate based on vitals)
2. Objective (O): Structured vital signs summary with abnormal values highlighted
3. Assessment (A): Clinical interpretation of deterioration pattern with MEWS context
4. Plan (P): Immediate interventions based on ICU Critical Care protocols

CONSTRAINTS:
- Use clinical terminology appropriate for ICU nursing documentation
- Prioritize patient safety and JCI compliance
- Include escalation criteria (when to notify physician)
- Keep total note under 200 words for efficiency
